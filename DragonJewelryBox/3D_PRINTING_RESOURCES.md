## Part Libraries â€” Find & Share Models

| Site | Best For | Link |
|------|----------|------|
| **Printables** (Prusa) | Largest free library, active community, contests | https://www.printables.com |
| **Thingiverse** | Legacy library, huge catalog | https://www.thingiverse.com |
| **MakerWorld** (Bambu) | Growing fast, good filtering, print profiles | https://makerworld.com |
| **Thangs** | AI-powered geometric search, aggregates multiple sites | https://thangs.com |
| **MyMiniFactory** | Curated, some paid/designer content | https://www.myminifactory.com |
| **Cults3D** | Mix of free and paid, designer marketplace | https://cults3d.com |

**Best combo for your workflow:**
- **Browse/download**: Printables + Thangs (Thangs searches across all sites)
- **Post your designs**: Printables (largest audience, supports 3MF uploads, good license options)
